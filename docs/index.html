Lyric Prompter

A native Android app that helps guitarists remember lyrics during performance.

Features

  • Offline speech recognition (Vosk) - no internet needed during performance
  • Automatic lyrics search
  • BPM lookup for count-in timing
  • Bluetooth earpiece support for discrete prompts

How It Works

The app uses voice recognition to track what you're singing and speaks prompts for upcoming lines through your earpiece. It follows YOUR tempo - no beat matching or karaoke-style timing.
